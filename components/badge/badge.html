Badge

Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component. It is used to highlight an item's status for quick recognition or notification.

Different Badge Types

Alerts can be used by including the badge keyword in the class followed by its varient name. There are 4 varients available which is BELL, MAIL, CART & AVATAR. Changing the varient name affects the icon scheme of the badge.
